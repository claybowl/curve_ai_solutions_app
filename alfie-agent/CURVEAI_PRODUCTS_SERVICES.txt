CURVE AI SOLUTIONS - PRODUCTS & SERVICES
=========================================

CORE PRODUCTS
-------------

1. AGENTIC AI SOLUTIONS (Custom AI Chatbots & Workflows)
   Description: Custom AI chatbots and workflow solutions that handle mundane, 
   repetitive tasks while understanding context and learning business needs.
   
   Features:
   - Context-aware AI agents
   - Personality customization to match company culture
   - Workflow automation
   - Integration with existing business systems
   - Learning capabilities that adapt to business needs
   
   Use Cases:
   - Customer service automation
   - Administrative task handling
   - Data analysis and integration
   - Process automation

2. AI CONSULTATION SERVICES
   Description: Expert guidance on implementing AI solutions for businesses.
   
   Services Include:
   - Business assessments
   - AI strategy development
   - Process automation consulting
   - Implementation guidance
   - ROI analysis
   
   Delivery Methods:
   - Online consultation requests
   - Email-based communication
   - Assessment tools
   - Follow-up support

3. AI ASSESSMENT TOOLS
   Description: AI-powered tools for evaluating business processes and 
   identifying opportunities for AI implementation.
   
   Features:
   - Automated business process evaluation
   - Opportunity identification
   - Customized recommendations
   - Results tracking and reporting
   - Email delivery of results

4. ROI CALCULATOR
   Description: Tool for calculating return on investment for AI implementations.
   
   Features:
   - AI-powered business profile generation (via OpenRouter)
   - Cost-benefit analysis
   - Customized ROI projections
   - Industry-specific calculations

5. PROMPT LIBRARY
   Description: Searchable library of AI prompts for various business tasks.
   
   Features:
   - Categorized prompts by use case
   - Search functionality
   - Tag-based organization
   - Best practices and examples
   - (Planned) User contributions with moderation

6. SOLUTIONS SHOWCASE
   Description: Display of successful AI solutions and case studies.
   
   Features:
   - Case study presentations
   - Industry-specific examples
   - Filter by industry or AI model
   - Success metrics and results
