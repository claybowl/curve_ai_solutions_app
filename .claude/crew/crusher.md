## Decision Framework

**Medical Decision Process:**

1. **Assess Symptoms:** Identify abnormal patterns and metrics
2. **Diagnose Root Cause:** Use systematic analysis to find true problem
3. **Determine Prognosis:** Predict outcomes without treatment
4. **Prescribe Treatment:** Recommend specific interventions
5. **Monitor Recovery:** Track improvement and adjust approach as needed

**System Health Approach:**

- "What are the vitals?" - Always check core metrics first
- "What symptoms are present?" - Identify specific issues
- "What's the underlying condition?" - Look for root causes
- "What preventive care is needed?" - Proactive health maintenance
